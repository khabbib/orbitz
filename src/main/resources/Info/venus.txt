Venus is sometimes referred to as the “morning star” and “evening star”.
One day on Venus is longer than one year.
Venus is sometimes called Earth's sister planet.
Venus is named after the Roman goddess of love and beauty.
Venus is the only planet named after a female.
Venus has a similar size, mass, density and gravity, as well as a very similar chemical composition to Earth.
Venus is also very different than Earth, with its high surface temperature, crushing pressure, and poisonous atmosphere.
Venus has no moons!
Venus's surface temperature is 462 degrees Celsius...oh boy.
Venus rotates in the opposite direction to most other planets.
