Uranus orbits the Sun on its side.
A Season on Uranus lasts one long day – 42 years.
Uranus has rings.
Uranus has 27 moons.
Uranus has winds within its atmosphere that can reach up to 250 m/s (900 km/h), and can generate anticyclonic storms.
The third-most-abundant component of Uranus’s atmosphere is methane (CH), which is what accounts for Uranus’ aquamarine color. 
Uranus was the first planet discovered in the modern age.
You can see Uranus without a telescope.
Uranus has only been visited once, by Voyager 2, 1986.
Assuming you could stand on Uranus, you would only experience about 89% the force of gravity.
